Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe on how to make Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Add Tomatoes and cook until softened
  3. Add carrots
  4. Cook spaghetti pasta
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. Cook until ready
